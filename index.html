Professional Profile

Dr. Lokesh Yadav is a consultant cardiothoracic and vascular surgeon at Nobel Medical College & Teaching Hospital, Biratnagar, Nepal. With over six years of clinical and academic experience, his expertise covers complex cardiac, thoracic, and vascular procedures. His work combines surgical precision with compassionate, patient-centered care.
